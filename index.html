introduction of the projector

My name is Dzunani Theo Mathevula, proudly born and raised in Bungeni Village under the leadership of Chief Given Bungeni. Growing up in a place rich with culture, rhythm, and storytelling shaped my deep love for music and the emotions it carries. Music has always been more than just sound to me it is a way people connect, celebrate life, and express their journeys. This passion inspired me to create this platform to share and celebrate one of the most powerful musical experiences of our time: the album Bab'Motha by Kabza De Small. As you explore the tracklist and take in the artwork of this incredible project, I invite you to feel the spirit of Amapiano and the creativity behind it. Let the music speak to you the same way it spoke to me and once you're done here, don't hesitate to go stream the album and experience its full magic for yourself.
